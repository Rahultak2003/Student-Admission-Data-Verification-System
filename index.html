Government Polytechnic College Jodhpur

Government Polytechnic College Jodhpur, established in 1958, is a renowned institution offering diverse engineering diploma courses with state-of-the-art facilities on a sprawling 227 bigha campus. Known for its excellence in Electronics and Information Technology, it plays a pivotal role in technical education and industrial growth in Rajasthan.

Award Ceremony of State Level Sports Competition Winners
@ GPC Jodhpur

At the State Level Polytechnic Sports Competition 2023, GPC Jodhpur excelled by winning three gold and three silver medals, totaling six medals. Today, Principal Shri Anshu Sahgal organized an award ceremony to honor all the athletes and accompanying staff for their remarkable achievements.

Massive Plantation Drive @ GPC Jodhpur

Government Polytechnic College Jodhpur is dedicated to environmental sustainability, regularly organizing tree plantation drives on its sprawling 227 bigha campus. These initiatives not only enhance the campus greenery but also promote ecological awareness among students and staff.

MoU between Lado CAD Technology and GPC Jodhpur

On 23rd May 2022, Government Polytechnic College Jodhpur and Government Polytechnic College Mandor signed an MoU with Lado CAD Technology. This partnership aims to enhance curriculum design, provide industrial training and tours, offer student internships and placements, and promote research, development, and skill development programs.
